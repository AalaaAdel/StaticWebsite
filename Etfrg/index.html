FAQs

How can I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets online through our website, at the cinema's box office, or through our mobile app.

Do you offer discounts for large groups?

Yes, we offer group discounts for parties of 10 or more. Contact our group sales department for more details and customized packages.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space for our patrons. Our parking facilities are well-lit and monitored for security.

Can I bring outside food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed in the theaters. However, we offer a variety of snacks and beverages at our concession stand.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we offer private event packages for special occasions, corporate gatherings, and film premieres. Contact our events team for more information.
