Genre

Running Time

115 Minutes

Description

Life circumstances push the football lover, Maged (Nour El Nabawy), to let go of his luxurious life and transfer from his private school to a public one. There, through his football skills, Maged is able to earn the respect of his new friends and joins their street football team. Together, they dream of joining “El Hareefa” school tournament to win its one million pound prize!
