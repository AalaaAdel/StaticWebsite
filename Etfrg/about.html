About Us

Etfarag Cinema is a brand new movie theater located in the heart of New Cairo. We strive to provide the ultimate cinematic experience to our audience, offering a wide selection of films across various genres.

Our state-of-the-art facilities feature cutting-edge audio and visual technology, ensuring that every moviegoer enjoys crystal-clear sound and stunning visuals. Whether you're a fan of action-packed blockbusters or heartwarming dramas, we have something for everyone.

At Etfarag Cinema, we are committed to excellence in customer service. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to assist you every step of the way, from purchasing tickets to finding the perfect seat in the theater.

Experience the magic of cinema like never before at Etfarag Cinema. Join us for an unforgettable journey through the world of movies and entertainment.
